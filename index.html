NoAdsDude

Websites for humans, not for advertisers.

NoAdsDude is a small family of websites built without ads, popups, cookie banners, or tracking. No analytics. No "sign up to read more." No 15-megabyte download just to check the weather.

Maybe this is the future of the internet. Maybe it's the past — Internet 1.0, or 0.5, depending on how grumpy you're feeling. Either way, I got tired of the bloat, so I started building.

Why bother

  1. Speed. A page should appear before your thumb finishes scrolling — not after megabytes of trackers and consent banners load first.
  2. Privacy by default. No cookies, no analytics, no fingerprinting. The site doesn't know who you are because it doesn't need to.
  3. One thing, done well. Each site does the one thing its name promises. No newsletter capture, no "related content," no engagement loop.

Privacy

This page makes zero third-party requests. No analytics, no cookies, no fingerprinting, no remote fonts, no embedded widgets. Fonts and stylesheets are served from noadsdude.com.

The only data stored locally is your theme preference, kept in your browser's localStorage (not a cookie).

Outbound links to sibling sites (noadsweather.com, noadssports.com, backonthemenu.com) and to Ko-fi take you off this site; their own privacy practices apply once you click.
