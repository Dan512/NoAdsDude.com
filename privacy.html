NoAdsDude

Privacy

This page (and every other NoAdsDude site) is built around one simple promise: no surveillance.

What this page does

The noadsdude.com landing page makes zero third-party requests. No analytics, no cookies, no fingerprinting, no remote fonts, no embedded widgets, no tag managers, no pixels. The only files loaded are served from noadsdude.com itself: HTML, CSS, three woff2 font files, and a tiny favicon.

The only data stored locally is your theme preference (light or dark), kept in your browser's localStorage. That is not a cookie — it is never sent to any server.

When you click an outbound link

Links to sibling sites (noadsweather.com, noadssports.com, backonthemenu.com) and to Ko-fi take you off noadsdude.com. Those destinations have their own privacy practices. Each sibling site discloses its own third-party calls; check their privacy panels for the details.

Open source

The NoAdsDude family is open source. See github.com/Dan512/noadsweather for a representative example of how these sites are built.
